Frequently Asked Questions

Is Balancc safe?

Yes. We use bank-grade encryption and only have read-only access to your accounts. We can never move or modify your money.

Does Balancc move or hold my money?

No. Balancc simply connects to your accounts to show you your real financial picture. Your money stays where it is.

Is Balancc free?

Early access is completely free. Premium features will roll out later.

How long does setup take?

Less than 3 minutes to connect your accounts and start seeing your true balance.
